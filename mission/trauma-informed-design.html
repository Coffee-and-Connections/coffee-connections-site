Safety by Design

Our architecture is our first act of care.

Clear Sightlines

Hyper-vigilance is exhausting. We designed our layout to eliminate "blind corners," allowing your nervous system to stand down[cite: 594].

Choice & Control

Trauma steals choice; we give it back. Choose between "protected back" seating or communal engagement[cite: 434, 590].

Acoustic Softening

Loud, sharp noises trigger startle responses. We utilize acoustic dampening to keep ambient noise warm and humming[cite: 18, 598].

Biophilic Elements

Connection to nature reduces cortisol. We integrate living plants and natural wood textures to ground the space visually[cite: 1704].
