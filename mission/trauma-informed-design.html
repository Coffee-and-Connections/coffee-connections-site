The Architecture of Care

Safety by Design

Our architecture is our first act of care.

01

Clear Sightlines

Hyper-vigilance is exhausting. We designed our layout to eliminate "blind corners," allowing the nervous system to stand down and feel secure in the environment.

02

Choice & Control

Trauma steals choice; we give it back. Our layout offers "protected back" seating for those needing security or communal tables for those seeking engagement.

03

Acoustic Softening

Sharp noises trigger startle responses. We utilize acoustic dampening and warm ambient sound to maintain a grounding atmosphere.

04

Biophilic Elements

Connection to nature reduces cortisol. We integrate living plants and natural wood textures to ground the space visually and physically.

Every corner of 216 E Overland is being re-imagined to serve the person first, the business second.
