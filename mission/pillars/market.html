Survivor-Owned Business

Kelsie E. Stark

Kelsie E. Stark is a passionate creative and entrepreneur dedicated to capturing the raw, authentic moments of life. Through her lens, she helps individuals and brands tell their unique stories with clarity and heart. Driven by a deep appreciation for human connection and community, Kelsie’s work goes beyond just taking photographs—it is about preserving the moments that matter most and empowering the people in front of her camera.

Veteran-Owned Business

Samantha Recker

Samantha Recker is a U.S. Army veteran, community leader, and the visionary founder of Recker’s Outreach and Reconnecting with the Disconnected (RWTD). Drawing on her military discipline and a profound commitment to service, Samantha has built a network designed to empower at-risk youth and veterans alike. Her mission is simple but critical: replace isolation with opportunity, mentorship, and a renewed sense of civic pride.
