The Genesis

Some stories are born from fire.

This space wasn't built for caffeine. It was built for those who found pain before they ever found safety.

Tony
“I’m almost done, I can't wait... I’m going to use my love for coffee and open a Coffee Shop!”
Marylou
“That is a good idea, why don't you tie it to mental health? We could have used a place like that!”
“That's an amazing idea. We can build a place that holds and honors Survivors!”

On the way home from group trauma therapy, a coffee shop became a bridge.

Honoring History: The 1920s Boiler as our center hearth.

We turned the heat back on.

We took the literal heart of 216 E Overland—an old coal boiler—and moved it to the center of our Game Zone. It’s no longer for coal; it’s a hearth for Tabletop Gaming, MTG, and D&D. It's where connection is forged.

Built with Guidance

NAMI El Paso serves as our Programmatic Advisor. Every pixel of this site and every corner of our building is refined through their mental health expertise to ensure a safe, resilient environment for the Borderplex.

This is just the beginning.
