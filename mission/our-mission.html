The Lenox Heritage

From Structure to Stewardship

Our restoration of 216 E Overland honors the 1920s architecture of Gustavus Trost. We are preserving the building's industrial heart—including the historic boiler which will serve as a visual centerpiece for our 7,000 sq ft community Game Zone.
