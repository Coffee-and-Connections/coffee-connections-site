The Survivor-First Environment

Led by Marylou Culpepper, the housing protocols are built on her "Lived Experience" as a survivor of Military Sexual Trauma (MST)[cite: 26, 30, 40]. We ensure junior soldiers have a designated voice in their reintegration[cite: 33].

  • Umbrella Protocol Wrap-around care that follows the resident from their first point of contact through full stability[cite: 32].
  • Sensory Regulation Lighting and acoustics designed to lower cortisol and support neurodivergent recovery.
  • Peer Recovery On-site "Rally Points" where shared lived experience becomes the foundation of stability[cite: 32].
