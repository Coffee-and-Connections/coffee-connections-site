The Language of Healing

In this space, words matter. While the term "victim" is a necessary legal definition describing an event that happened to a person, we choose to focus on the journey that follows.

We use the term "Survivor."

A survivor is defined not by the harm inflicted upon them, but by their capacity to persist, adapt, and reclaim their life. By choosing this language, we honor the agency, strength, and resilience of every individual who shares their story here.

These are their pages. This is their truth.
