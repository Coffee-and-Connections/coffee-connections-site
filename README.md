# 🔒 Coffee & Connections | El Paso Borderplex
**A Vertically Integrated Ecosystem for Survivor Stability & Community Resilience.**

## 🏙️ Project Vision
Coffee & Connections is a dual-engine social enterprise designed to bridge the gap between crisis and stability. Located at Overland, this project integrates a trauma-informed "Sanctuary" Café with transitional safe-haven housing and a digital economic incubator for local survivors and veterans.

## 🏗️ Architectural Structure
The ecosystem is organized into three strategic pillars to reflect our physical and social infrastructure:

* **/cafe (The Economic Engine):** Features the Sanctuary Menu, the "Sanctuary Pour" pay-it-forward model, and the **Resilience Market Incubator**—a digital directory and web-hosting platform accelerating Veteran-owned, Survivor-owned, and MilSpouse businesses.
* **/mission (The Social Heart):** Contains the Genesis story, the Healing in Motion interactive archive, and the Own Your Truth submission portal.
* **/overland (The Strategic Prospectus):** Housing the "Architecture of Stability" design philosophy and the Bridge @ Overland residential pitch.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack: The "Dual-Tower" Architecture
To ensure survivor safety and data integrity, the site utilizes a split-API infrastructure managed via a central `config.js` gateway:

* **The Vault (Secure Tower):** A high-security environment (Node.js/Express/MongoDB) handling survivor narratives and crisis resources. Includes an Interactive Submission Engine with color-coded advocacy identifiers.
* **Public Community Tower:** Handles the Resilience Market partner profiles, menu data, and frontend community engagement.
* **Design System:** A custom "Sanctuary Palette" built on CSS Variables, engineered for low-glare, sensory-safe user experiences (Dark Mode default).

## 🛡️ Trauma-Informed Design System (NAMI-Aligned)
The visual interface is engineered for "Cognitive Ease" to support users who may be experiencing sensory overload or trauma-related stress.

* **Typography:** Montserrat (Architectural Stability) and Open Sans (Humanist Legibility).
* **The Safety Buffer:** A global 24px (0.25in) padding is enforced to prevent visual crowding and provide psychological boundaries.
* **Identity Tags:** Integrated color-coded advocacy identifiers (#Teal for SA, #Purple for DV, #Green for PTSD, #Orange for Community) to foster immediate community recognition.
