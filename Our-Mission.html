“Coffee & Connections is a sanctuary—where safety lives before healing begins.”

We didn't build this to serve coffee. We built it because too many of us found pain before we ever found safety.

Marylou survived trauma inside a system that tried to silenced her. Tony almost died at his own hand to show how deeply it was broken.

This space was born from that fire—from stories no one wanted to hear, and lives no one cared to try to save.
