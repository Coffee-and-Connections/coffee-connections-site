Our Mission

Through my own journey with PTSD and mental health struggles, I found a purpose rooted in presence, connection, and care.

Coffee & Connections exists to remind you: It’s okay NOT to be okay.

Here, you’ll find a warm cup of coffee, kind people to connect with, a game to play, or simply a quiet corner to reflect with a good book.

We don’t let people slip through the cracks—we extend hands, offer ears, and lend voices. Because at Coffee & Connections, radical welcome isn’t just a phrase…

It’s a promise.

—Tony Sorensen
Founder, Coffee & Connections
