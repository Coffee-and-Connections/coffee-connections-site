The Bridge @ Overland

A Vertically Integrated Ecosystem for Survivor Stability.

01

Ground Floor: The Economic Engine

The Sanctuary Café: A public-facing social enterprise providing job training and immediate community integration. Residents have priority access to living-wage employment in a secure, trauma-informed environment.

02-03

Upper Floors: Secure Safe Havens

8–12 fully furnished transitional units designed for survivors of DV and SA. Featuring pet-friendly policies, private amenities, and keycard-secured elevator access to ensure absolute safety and peace of mind.

B

The Basement: Restoration Zone

A sound-dampened decompression area featuring art therapy studios and a podcasting suite. This space serves as a "Rally Point" for peer-led recovery and youth programming.

The Collaborative Power

Our model integrates the best of El Paso's social infrastructure to ensure long-term tenant success and building stability.
